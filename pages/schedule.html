Full Event Breakdown

Check out the detailed breakdown of all scheduled events happening at MrBeast IRL Challenge.

🔹 12:00 PM – Gates Open, Carnival Begins 🎡

Attendees enter the MrBeast IRL Festival Grounds at Ontario Place. Fans can explore interactive booths, food stalls, and sponsored zones.

VIP & Press Check-in:

  • Media Lounge Access at the Prime Video Tent.
  • Press receives event swag, media passes, and early challenge previews.

🔹 1:00 PM – Live Challenge Rounds Begin 🏆

Featured Challenges:

  • “Last to Leave” Mega Challenge – Contestants compete to win $50,000 by being the last to exit a confined challenge zone.
  • Money Machine Madness – Step inside a cash tornado! Grab as much money as possible within 60 seconds.
  • Mystery Box Showdown – Choose wisely! You could win $5 or $5,000.

Celebrity Participants: MrBeast, Logan Paul, Airrack, and other YouTubers will participate.

🔹 3:00 PM – Meet & Greet with Influencers 🎤

Exclusive for VIP & Press Members. Get up close with MrBeast, Logan Paul, and special guests.

Media Opportunities:

  • Private Meet & Greet Zone for media & VIP fans.
  • One-on-one media interviews with creators & sponsors.

🔹 4:00 PM – Charity Challenge (Make-A-Wish Fundraiser) 💙

For every round won, MrBeast donates $10,000 to Make-A-Wish Canada. A surprise audience member will also get to participate for a charity jackpot!

🔹 6:00 PM – MrBeast Unfiltered Exclusive Screening 🎬

World-exclusive preview of MrBeast Unfiltered (Prime Video). VIPs and press get first-look footage before release.

🔹 7:00 PM – Grand Finale Challenge ($100,000 Cash Prize) 💰

The moment everyone’s waiting for! Contestants must complete a series of endurance & skill-based tasks to win $100,000, handed over by MrBeast himself.

🔹 9:00 PM – Live DJ & Fireworks 🎆

A live DJ set followed by an epic fireworks show to close out the festival.
